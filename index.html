ABOUT

Central School is an institution with international standard in education, co-scholastic activities, sports, infrastructure and facilities that embrace the demands of modern curriculum.


SYSTEM

We follow the CCE system divised by CBSE. This evaluation is based on scholastic performance leading to all round development of the child. Under this scheme the scholastic part is evaluated by the formative and Summative assessment of the performance in the core subject. Remedial coaching is undertaken on a need basis. Periodical interaction with parents make this campus an exemplary one and their suggestions are accepted in a sporty way.


our mission

The guiding beacons of our family - my mother and father - have set a mission for me and my offspring to follow the path of truth and engage in charitable works with an aim to appease the hunger and needs of the downtrodden in our area. This mission is blessed with means from their blood and sweat. We the present governing body shall do everything with in our reach to fulfill their cherished goals.
